Tax Benefits & Compliance

Every Way to Give — 80G, 12A, CSR & FCRA

Mandal Mahajan (Panjrapole) is a fully registered public charitable trust. Donations from individuals and companies — including CSR and overseas (FCRA) funds — are all welcome, with full tax benefits.

80G

Your donation is 80G tax-exempt — individual and company donors can claim income-tax deduction. PAN is required on the receipt.

12A

Registered under Section 12A as a recognised, tax-exempt charitable institution.

CSR

Companies can contribute their CSR funds to the panjrapole. CSR Reg. No. 00036095.

FCRA

Approved to receive foreign / overseas donations under FCRA. Reg. No. 041910303.

Registered Public Charitable Trust — Reg. No. F-462 · PAN AAATS6194D. Individual daan, company CSR funds and FCRA (overseas) contributions are all accepted.
