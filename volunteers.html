Why Join

Benefits & Responsibilities

Benefits

  • Hands-on experience in animal welfare and veterinary care
  • Certificate of volunteer service recognized by partner institutions
  • Training workshops on cattle health and sustainable farming
  • Community of like-minded compassionate individuals
  • Flexible scheduling – weekday and weekend shifts available
  • Meals and accommodation for long-term residential volunteers

Responsibilities

  • Commit to minimum 4 hours per week (or agreed schedule)
  • Follow safety protocols and supervisor instructions
  • Treat all animals with respect, patience, and gentleness
  • Maintain cleanliness and hygiene standards in all areas
  • Report any health concerns or incidents immediately
