Your time and care can make a real difference. Join the sevaks who keep the panjrapole running.

Get Involved

Ways to Help

Daily Care & Feeding

Help with feeding, cleaning sheds and looking after the cattle under the caretakers' guidance.

Rescue Support

Lend a hand when stray or injured cattle need to be brought in for shelter and treatment.

Outreach & Awareness

Spread the word about gauseva and jivdaya in the community and at local events.

Media & Documentation

Capture photos and stories from the panjrapole and help manage its social media.

Fodder & Fields

Assist with fodder, gaushala upkeep and the everyday work of keeping the grounds in order.

Office & Admin

Help with records, donor thank-yous, event planning and day-to-day administration.

What to Expect

Volunteering With Us

What you gain

  • The joy and peace of hands-on gauseva
  • Hands-on experience in animal care alongside our caretakers
  • A welcoming community of like-minded, compassionate people
  • Flexible timings — join on weekdays, weekends or festivals
  • A deeper connection to the tradition of jivdaya

What we ask

  • Treat every animal with patience, respect and gentleness
  • Follow the caretakers' guidance and safety instructions
  • Help keep the sheds and surroundings clean
  • Let us know in advance when you plan to come
