Best Practices

Sustainable Farming Practices

🌱 Organic Fodder Cultivation

Growing nutrient-rich fodder without chemical pesticides, using crop rotation and vermicompost for soil health.

💧 Water Conservation

Rainwater harvesting, drip irrigation for fodder fields, and recycled water systems for shelter cleaning.

♻️ Zero-Waste Gaushala

Converting cattle dung to biogas and organic manure, creating a closed-loop sustainable ecosystem.
