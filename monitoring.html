Daily Rounds

Looking After the Herd

Caretakers walk the sheds morning and evening, checking each animal, refilling water and flagging anything that needs the vet.

  • Morning and evening feeding, every day of the year
  • Fresh drinking water kept topped up across all sheds
  • Sick and injured cattle moved to the care shed
  • Old and weak animals given softer feed and shade
  • Sheds cleaned and bedding maintained regularly
