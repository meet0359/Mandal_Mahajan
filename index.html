What we do

Caring for every animal

From rescue to lifelong shelter, here is how the panjrapole looks after the cattle in its care.

Rescue & Shelter

We take in abandoned, stray, injured and aged cattle and give them a safe, permanent home for the rest of their lives.

Veterinary Treatment

Wounds, illness and weakness are treated on site, with regular health checks and care for animals that need it most.

Daily Feeding

Green and dry fodder, water and mineral feed are provided every day of the year, including for old and unproductive cattle.

Gaushala Tradition

Cow dung and gomutra are used for organic manure and traditional products, keeping the gaushala self-reliant.

Community Seva

Devotees and volunteers join in feeding, festivals and gauseva — keeping a centuries-old tradition alive together.

Sponsor a Cow

Support the upkeep of a cow through gau-grass daan and sponsorship that funds her feed and care year-round.
