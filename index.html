What We Do

Featured Services

Comprehensive care programs designed for the wellbeing of every animal in our sanctuary.

🏥

Rescue & Rehabilitation

Emergency rescue operations for injured, abandoned, and distressed cattle with full medical rehabilitation support.

🌱

Sustainable Farming

Promoting eco-friendly gaushala practices, organic fodder cultivation, and biogas energy from cattle waste.

🔬

Research & Education

Conducting veterinary research and community workshops on cattle health, nutrition, and welfare standards.

📡

Health Monitoring

Real-time tracking of cattle health, feeding schedules, vaccinations, and GPS-based pasture management.

🤝

Volunteer Programs

Engaging passionate individuals in daily care, outreach, and awareness campaigns across rural communities.

💝

Adoption & Sponsorship

Connect with rescued cattle through sponsorship programs that fund their lifelong care and nourishment.
