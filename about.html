About Mandal Mahajan

A legacy of compassion, research, and unwavering dedication to cattle welfare since 2010.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and provide lifelong care for cattle while promoting sustainable gaushala practices, community education, and veterinary research that advances animal welfare standards across India.

🔭 Vision

A world where every cattle life is valued, protected, and cared for with dignity — where communities embrace compassionate farming and zero tolerance for animal neglect and cruelty.

Our Journey

History Timeline

2010

Foundation

Mandal Mahajan established with 12 rescued cattle and a small team of 5 dedicated caregivers in Satara district.

2014

Gaushala Expansion

Expanded to a 50-acre sanctuary with modern veterinary facilities, biogas plant, and organic fodder farm.

2017

Research Division Launched

Partnered with agricultural universities to begin indigenous cattle nutrition and health research programs.

2020

Digital Monitoring System

Implemented GPS tracking, health dashboards, and vaccination management for all sanctuary cattle.

2023

500+ Rescues Milestone

Celebrated rescuing over 500 cattle with a 98% recovery rate and 186 active volunteers.

2026

Regional Leadership

Recognized as a model gaushala by the State Animal Welfare Board, training 20+ organizations nationwide.
